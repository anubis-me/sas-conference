Welcome Message

“Dear Colleagues and Friends,"

Welcome ! The organizing committee warmly invites you to participate in the Emerging Trends in the Catalysis – 2020 to be held in Vellore Institute of Technology – Vellore campus, on January 6-8 2020. This conference is organized in association with Royal Society of Chemistry (RSC), UK. This catalysis meet will be inaugurated by Nobel Laureate Dr. Robert Grubbs, who has been awarded Nobel Prize in chemistry for his pioneering contributions in the field of olefin metathesis in the year 2005. This conference aims at bringing scientists, in a single platform, working in the broad area of catalysis which includes organometallic catalysts, bio-catalyst, catalyst used in bioremediation, homogeneous and heterogeneous catalysis This conference also provides avenues to bring participants together from academia, industry, and research institutes. This will also be an ideal platform for young researchers and mentoring students to cultivate research culture to create a society of intellectuals and visionaries. Welcome on behalf of the ETC-2020 Organizing Committee and it’s sponsors. Your participation and valuable contributions to this conference will sure make this event a grand success. We look forward to interacting with you all in Vellore.

I look forward to your participation, and wish everyone have an unforgettable stay at Vellore Institute of Technology, Vellore, India.

Best Wishes,

Dr. A. Mary Saral
Dean, SAS,
Chair Person
